ORIGIN LOG

The Horror Pop Extravaganza is a weekly podcast dedicated to celebrating horror culture and the Beautiful Souls who love it. Each episode blends nostalgia, humor, and deep dives into the eerie corners of pop culture.

Hosted by Pops Ghostly, Lil Duke Spooky, Chelsey “CeeWolf” Graves, and B-Money, the show delivers laughs, chills, and the occasional guest from the world of all things spooky.

OBSERVED BEHAVIOR

🎬 THE MOVIE

Each week we explore a horror film from across the genre — breaking down the scares, the story, and the unforgettable moments that define it. From slashers to supernatural, nothing is off limits.

🌙 PAUSE FOR THE LIVING

After the film, we take a step back. Pops shares real-world reflections rooted in positivity, mindfulness, and connection — because horror fans deserve light as much as darkness.

DIRECTIVE

We value your time and aim to create content that is engaging, nostalgic, and meaningful. Our goal is to build a community where horror fans can connect, laugh, and feel at home.

Peace and calm we leave you with.

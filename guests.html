Meet Our Hosts

Pops Ghostly

The heart and mindful spirit of HPE. Pops leads the charge each week, guiding listeners through horror films and life reflections alike. During “Pause for the Living,” Pops brings grounded, real-world insight rooted in love, kindness, and connection.

Lil Duke Spooky

Bringing high energy and fearless reactions, Lil Duke Spooky dives headfirst into every horror flick. Expect bold takes, contagious enthusiasm, and a deep love for all things creepy and chaotic.

Chelsey “CeeWolf” Graves

CeeWolf delivers sharp analysis, unapologetic opinions, and a passion for the darker corners of horror cinema. Whether it’s classic slashers or psychological thrillers, she keeps the conversation engaging and thought-provoking.

B-Money

The wildcard of the crew. B-Money keeps things lively and unpredictable, blending humor with real talk. When the conversation takes a wild turn, B-Money is right there steering into the madness.
